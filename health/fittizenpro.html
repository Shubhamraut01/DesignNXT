Project overview

Fitizen Pro app can perform various functions such as allowing users to set fitness goals, tracking caloric intake, gathering workout ideas, and sharing progress on social media to facilitate healthy behavior change. They can be used as a platform to promote healthy behavior change with personalized workouts, fitness advice and nutrition plans. Fitizen Pro app can work in conjunction with wearable devices to synchronize their health data to third-party devices for easier accessibility.

The problem:
The popular fitness apps does not have various workout videos that users can follow to exercise..
The goal:
Design an app for Fitizen Pro that provide the user with instructions and examples of one or more types of exercise, physical activity, nutritional programs, or some other fitness topic.
